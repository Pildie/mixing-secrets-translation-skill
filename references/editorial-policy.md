# Editorial Policy

1. **Language Register:** Contemporary Brazilian Portuguese (standard). Not archaic, not overly slangy.
2. **Pessoa do Leitor:** Always use "você" (direct appeal). Avoid "o leitor" or "o engenheiro."
3. **Pessoa do Autor:** Use "eu" (natural first-person).
4. **Bold/Italics:** Only use where the original uses.
5. **Quotes:** Professional quotes from engineers (Chuck Ainlay, Serban Ghenea, etc.) must be fluent, not literal.
6. **Album Titles:** Keep in English. Optional informal translation in parentheses on first occurrence.
7. **Capitalization:**
   - Chapter/Section titles: PT-BR rules (only first word and proper nouns).
   - Generic terms: lower case (compressor, equalizador).
   - Brands: follow official brand capitalization (Neve, SSL, Focusrite).
